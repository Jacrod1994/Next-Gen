Welcome to NextGen

NextGen is an excellent opportunity for companies to recruit students from the School of Computing for a year placement or shorter work experiences, it also puts your organisation in front of our students as a potential employer.

We have successfully placed students in internationally renowned companies including IBM, Microsoft, Schlumberger, Framestore, Disney, Xerox, HP, DuPont and Nissan, in addition to small to medium businesses.

Why NextGen is good for your business

It's a perfect opportunity for your business to locate and connect with up and coming talent early to secure a student placement or internship.

We can support the recruitment process for you.

Build up your network of professional contacts and develop relationships with established and emerging businesses.

Work experience programmes

There are two work experiences programmes that your company can choose from:

A placement year is an opportunity for your company to address a specific project or series of projects that requires a fresh insight and extra pair of hands. It's also an opportunity for your business to experience how a new graduate would work in your organisation.

A short internship (for instance over the summer). The School actively encourages IT related placements as they will boost the students' career aspirations and strengthen links between industry and the School and provide temporary help to your business.

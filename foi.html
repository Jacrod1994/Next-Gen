Freedom of information

The University is a public authority under the Freedom of Information Act 2000. Requests for information under the Act should be made to the University Secretary's Department or by E: foi@tees.ac.uk.

The Freedom of Information Act 2000 provides a general right of access to information held by public bodies, including universities, subject to certain exemptions. The Environmental Information Regulations 2004 work on a similar principle on issues affecting the environment. The information here applies to both legislation.

You should make your request in writing and send it to: Department for Quality and Governance, Teesside University, Middlesbrough, TS1 3BA, or email foi@tees.ac.uk. You should include your name, a correspondence address and as much detail as possible about the information you require
